ðŸ’° Commission Calculation (Including/Excluding GST)
It's crucial to distinguish between commission amounts excluding and including GST. Nestaway charges owner commission inclusive of GST, calculated as: Commission + 18% of the commission. For example, if the commission is 10%, then 18% of 10 is 1.8, making the commission including GST 11.8% (10 + 1.8). Contracts describe commission excluding GST; ensure accurate calculations are communicated to owners.
