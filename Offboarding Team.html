Owner Offboarding Process

Step Action Description
Introduction Once the owner decides to terminate the contract with Nestaway, the offboarding team manages the process, including property refurbishment, financial settlements, and handing over the property.
1 Receive Offboarding Request Upon receiving the offboarding request, access the 'New Flow' system using the house ID to initiate the offboarding process.
2 Initiate Retention Task Begin the retention task promptly to attempt to retain the owner, as losing a property impacts the business.
3 Contact Owner Immediately call the owner to understand the reason behind their decision to terminate the contract.
4 Identify Issues If the decision is due to service dissatisfaction, work to address and resolve these issues to restore the owner's confidence.
5 Consult City Head If the owner wishes to occupy the house themselves or there is a policy dispute, seek guidance from the city head on how to proceed.
6 Implement Retention Strategies Communicate and implement any suggestions from the city head to persuade the owner to continue with Nestaway.
7 Close Retention Task If the house is retained, immediately close the retention task in the 'New Flow' system and mark the status as 'RETAINED'.
8 Proceed with Offboarding If retention efforts fail, report to the city head and, upon approval, close the retention task marking it as 'COULD NOT RETAIN', providing detailed reasons for offboarding.
